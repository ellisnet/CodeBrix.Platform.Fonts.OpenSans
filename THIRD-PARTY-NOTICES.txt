THIRD-PARTY SOFTWARE NOTICES AND INFORMATION

CodeBrix.Platform.Fonts.OpenSans is a CodeBrix-family redistribution of the
Open Sans font family, packaged as a .NET 10 NuGet library. The package
incorporates third-party material from two distinct sources:

  1. Uno.Fonts.OpenSans 2.8.1 (Apache-2.0) — the original packaging shape,
     `.targets` MSBuild logic, `.uprimarker` mechanism, and `.ttf.manifest`
     JSON format, all designed and originally published by Uno Platform Inc.
  2. The Open Sans font family (SIL OFL 1.1) — the actual `.ttf` font
     files themselves, originally designed by Steve Matteson and currently
     maintained by the Google Fonts team as the Open Sans Project.

Each of these is reproduced under the terms of its own license. The full
SIL Open Font License Version 1.1 text is bundled with this repository as
`OFL.txt` at the repository root and is also packaged inside the produced
NuGet under the same name.
